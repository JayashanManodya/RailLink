About RailLink

RailLink is a modern train scheduling and booking platform designed to make Sri Lankan rail travel simple, secure, and delightful. We connect passengers with reliable train services across the island, providing real-time schedules, easy booking, and comprehensive travel information.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a train ticket?

Simply search for trains using our search form, select your preferred schedule, and complete the booking process with your passenger details.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking from the "My Bookings" section, provided it's not too close to the departure time.

How do I change my password?

Go to your profile page and use the "Change Password" section to update your password securely.

What if I have technical issues?

Contact our support team via email or phone, and we'll help you resolve any technical problems quickly.
